Recommended venues

These are the top venues we recommend for your challenge, chosen for the quality of their assault course and helpfulness of their staff. You should contact them to discuss your precise requirements before booking or committing to your challenge. The venues are usually more than willing to help you create the best challenge possible for you or your cause.

 

The best place to find additional female marshals is through the OCR Volunteers Facebook group.

 

Example of potential arrangements to be discussed with venue (based on South East venue)

England

London and south east

Camelot Events

www.camelotevents.co.uk

01306 888067

theteam@camelotevents.co.uk

Camelot Events at Henfold Lakes offer an excellent assault course set in beautiful countryside. Lots of wet and muddy obstacles to overcome, creating an amazing adventure all around. Brilliant staff. Very helpful when organising charity events. Female instructors available.

Birmingham and midlands

Warped Activity Centre

www.warpedsports.com/assault-course

01902 455455

events@warpedsports.com

Warped Sports promise adrenaline filled activities set out over their woodland site, to ensure that every participant is pushed mentally and physically.

Bristol and south west

Mendip Outdoor Pursuits

www.mendipoutdoorpursuits.co.uk

01934 834877

info@mendip.me

Mendip Outdoor Pursuits offer a challenging assault course comprising two parallel routes of obstacles including walls, balance beams, seesaws, mud crawl nets, under/over bars, underground tunnels. tyre climbs, river crossings and other surprises. Suitable for all abilities.

Scotland

Dundee

Scottish Assault Courses

www.scottishassaultcourses.com

07775 777155

dundee@scottishassaultcourses.com

Located half way between Perth and Dundee, this assault course is based at the Errol Airfield site.

Edinburgh

Scottish Assault Courses

www.scottishassaultcourses.com

07860 650565

dundee@scottishassaultcourses.com

Includes a mile long assault course with all the usual features such as muddy obstacles, tunnels, trenches, crawl nets as well structures including walls, monkey bars, rope swings and much more!

Glasgow​

Craufurdland Estate

www.craufurdland.co.uk

0785 822177

info@craufurdland.co.uk

Famous for Muddy Trials, this venue offers guided treks around the estate. Depending on the fitness level of the group and how adventurous you are feeling, the trek can be geared to your needs.

Wales

Cardiff

Taff Valley Activity Centre

www.adventurewales.co.uk

02920 831658

info@taffvalley.co.uk

Taff Valley Activity Centre promise a thrilling woodland assault course, covering around 2km of farmland. Participants must traverse woodland, streams and open fields. The obstacles will cover running, climbing, jumping, crawling, wading and balancing elements.

Carmarthen

Morfa Bay Adventure

www.morfabay.com

01994 453588

enquiries@morfabay.com

Set within 3 acres of on-site woodland, this mud assault course offers a fun filled challenge for all ages and abilities. Getting wet and muddy is all part of the fun in this physical activity, as you tackle all of the obstacles in front of you and have a great laugh at the same time.

Swansea

Clyne Farm

www.clynefarm.com

01792 403333

hello@clynefarm.com

A very muddy assault course, in which participants must climb over obstacles and crawl on the ground. It can be completed by anyone over the age of eight years and is suitable for all ages and abilities.

Step 1: Create your team

Talk to family, friends and colleagues, and persuade them to take on the challenge with you.
You will need a minimum of 10 women or girls in your team.

Step 2: Book your venue

Book your team's session at your preferred venue for the date and time of your choice.
Phone or email them to discuss your precise requirements.
Remember to request female marshals for your challenge.
